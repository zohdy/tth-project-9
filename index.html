Who Am I ?

I'm Peter, based in Copenhagen, Denmark. I recently finished a BSc in Computer Science, now specializing in web development.

I'm obsessed with small details and extremely persistent when it comes to solving strange problems, especially late at night.

When I'm not writing code I'm probably working on music-related stuff or out spinning records.

Background

With over a decade of working in the Music Industry as a consultant, DJ, producer and radio host, I've always been curious about the technology side of things. I started writing code to be able to build and manipulate synthesizers and drum machines. Slowly, I found myself spending a lot more time coding than writing music and decided to solely focus on programming.

My background in the Music Business is very different from any programmer that I know of. I've been able to develop considerable skills in regards to working with people from a variety of cultural and socio-economic backgrounds. This has equipped me with strong communication and collaboration skills and a practical approach to business.
